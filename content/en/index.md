You can integrate with our online payments APIs in several ways. These range from using a pre-built integration, to building your own UI for full control over your checkout experience.

## Types of integrations:

<dl>
    <dt><strong>Checkout</strong></dt>
    <dd>Embed a payment interface in your website, with some customization. <a href="/checkout/overview">Docs</a></dd>
	<dt><strong>Via plugin</strong></dt>
	<dd>Use one of our plugins. We support WooCommerce, Shopify, Prestashop, or Magento. <a href="https://easypay.pt">Docs</a></dd></dd>
	<dt><strong>API Only</strong></dt>
	<dd>Create your own UI and process transactions using our API. <a href="https://easypay.pt">Docs</a></dd></dd>
</dl>
